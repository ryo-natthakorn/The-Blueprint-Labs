Private dining & events

A room off the kitchen,
a night that's yours.

Our private dining room sits just off the main kitchen — same menu, same sourcing, a table that's actually yours for the night. You'll hear the pass, smell the wood grill, and watch your dishes leave the kitchen first.

  • A welcome pour at the bar while your table is finished.
  • The night's menu chalked on your own wall — dishes named for the occasion, if you like.
  • Chef's tasting for parties of six or more: seven courses, decided that morning at the dock.
  • Wine pairings or the zero-proof flight, matched course by course.
  • One long table, up to fourteen — no room fee Tuesday through Thursday.

How it works

Three emails, no contracts-by-the-pound

1 · Tell us the occasion. Date, headcount, and anything the table needs to know about — allergies, anniversaries, someone who claims they hate fish. Use the enquiry form or just call.

2 · We hold the room. You'll hear back within a day with a menu sketch for the season and a simple per-head figure. One deposit holds the night.

3 · The tide does the rest. The final menu is written the morning of, same as the dining room. That's not a caveat — it's the reason to come.
