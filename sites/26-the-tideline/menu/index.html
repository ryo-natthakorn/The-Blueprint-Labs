Tonight's menu · today

Written this morning,
gone by midnight.

The menu is rewritten daily around the morning's landing and what the farms cut this week. What you see below is tonight; tomorrow the water decides again. Dietary tags sit beside every dish — and tell us about any allergy, we take it seriously.
