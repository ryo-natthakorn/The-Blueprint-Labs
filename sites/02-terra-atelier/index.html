陶 Terra Atelier
raw clay

土から

Every vessel
begins as earth.

Scroll slowly. The wheel is already turning.

中心

Centering

The clay fights you at first. Steady pressure, wet palms — until it runs true and silent under the hand. Nothing can be built on a lump that wobbles.

開く

Opening

Thumbs find the center and press down. A floor forms. The walls remember every hesitation, so we don't hesitate.

引き上げ

Pulling up

Three pulls, no more. Knuckle outside, fingertips inside, rising together. The vessel grows taller and thinner with each pass — this is the moment most pots die.

形

Shaping

A rib against the shoulder coaxes the belly outward. The curve you're watching was decided a thousand pots ago; the hand just remembers it.

窯

Fired & finished

Glazed in our studio celadon, kiln-fired to 1280°C. Drag the vessel to turn it in your hands.

Terra Atelier is a fictional studio — a Blueprint Labs capability study.
